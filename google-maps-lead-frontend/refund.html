Refund Policy

Last Updated: February 10, 2026

Our Refund Commitment

We want you to be happy with Promptix. If our service fails technically, we’ll make it right by restoring your credits. Because searches consume live resources, we generally don’t offer cash refunds once data has been delivered.

1. Credit Restorations

If a search fails due to a technical error on our platform (e.g., the extractor crashes or no data is returned), the credits used for that search will be automatically restored to your account.

2. Refund Eligibility

We provide a "Free" tier so you can test the service before buying. Once you purchase a paid plan:

  • Unused Credits: You may request a refund within 7 days of purchase if no credits have been consumed from your new balance.
  • Consumed Credits: Once credits have been successfully used to extract data, those portions of the payment are non-refundable.

3. Processing Refunds

Refunds are processed back to your original payment method via Razorpay. Depending on your bank, it may take 5-10 business days for the funds to appear in your account.

4. How to Request

Need help with a refund or credit restoration? Contact us at infopromptix@gmail.com with your account email and transaction ID. We're here to help!
