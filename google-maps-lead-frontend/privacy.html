Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 10, 2026

The "Too Long; Didn't Read" Version

We respect your privacy. We only collect the bare minimum information needed to run Promptix. We don't sell your data, we don't track you across the web, and we believe the leads you extract belong to you, not us.

1. What we collect (and why)

To provide a great experience, we only collect the information necessary to make Promptix work for you:

  • Identity: Your full name and email address so we can identify you and provide support.
  • Login Credentials: Your password is hashed and stored using industry-standard security, meaning we (and anyone else) can never see your actual password.
  • Usage Data: We keep track of your search history (keywords and locations) and credit usage so your account stays in sync and you can re-run searches easily.
  • Payment Identifiers: When you upgrade, we receive transaction IDs from Razorpay to confirm your purchase. We do not store, and cannot see, your credit or debit card details. All actual payment processing happens on Razorpay's secure infrastructure.

2. How we use your data

We use the information we collect solely to provide, maintain, and improve our services. This includes:

  • Providing you with search results and managing your search history.
  • Updating your credit balance after successful searches.
  • Notifying you about important service updates or account issues.

Most importantly: We do not sell your personal data or search history to anyone. Period.

3. Public Data & Third-Party Services

We use Apify, a trusted third-party scraping service provider, to help collect business information from Google Maps. Here is how that works:

  • Public Sources Only: We only extract info that is already publicly available on Google Maps (like business names, addresses, and phone numbers).
  • No Private Access: We never access private files, personal emails, or restricted social media profiles.
  • Third-Party Policies: While we choose our partners carefully, these services follow their own privacy policies for how they handle data processing.

4. Security Measures

Your security is our priority. We take reasonable and appropriate measures to protect your data:

  • Hashing: As mentioned, passwords are never stored in plain text. They are "hashed" or encrypted so they are unreadable even to us.
  • Encryption: We use industry-standard HTTPS encryption for all data moving between your browser and our servers.
  • Locked Down: Our database and servers use strict access controls to prevent unauthorized access.

5. Your Rights & Data Control

You have full control over your information. At any time, you can exercise the following rights:

  • Right to Deletion: You can request that we delete your account entirely. Once deleted, this action cannot be undone.
  • Right to Data Removal: You can request that we purge specific search history or usage data from our systems while keeping your account active.
  • Right to Access: You can ask for a copy of the personal data we hold about you.

6. Privacy Concerns & Contact

If you have any questions about this policy, or if you wish to exercise any of your rights (such as account deletion), please contact our privacy team directly at infopromptix@gmail.com.

We aim to respond to all privacy-related requests within 48 hours.
